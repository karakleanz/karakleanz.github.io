How it works

KaraKleanz is a premiere commercial and domestic cleaning service. We redefine the cleaning experience into an art of spatial sanctity.

Pick Service

Choose from: Regular Clean, Deep Clean or End‑of‑Tenancy. Each service includes a clear checklist so you always know what’s covered.

Book Slot

Select a date and time that works for you. You’ll receive a confirmation email and reminders before your appointment.

We Arrive

Your cleaner shows up on time with professional products and equipment. All staff are vetted, trained, and insured for your peace of mind.

We Clean

We follow your chosen checklist, focus on your priorities, and leave your space fresh, tidy, and spotless.

Review & Rebook

Once the job is done, you can rate your clean, request adjustments, or book recurring visits.

Services

Standard Clean

A thorough top‑to‑bottom clean designed to keep your home fresh, tidy, and comfortable. Ideal for regular weekly or fortnightly maintenance.

Includes:

  • Dusting all surfaces
  • Steaming carpets
  • Vacuuming & mopping floors
  • Kitchen wipe‑down
  • Bathroom clean & sanitisation
  • Emptying bins
  • Light tidying
Perfect for busy households, professionals, and anyone who wants a consistently clean space.

Deep Clean

A more intensive clean that targets built‑up dirt, grime, and hard‑to‑reach areas. Recommended every 2–3 months or before special occasions.

Includes everything in Regular Clean, plus:

  • Skirting boards & door frames
  • Behind appliances (where accessible)
  • Limescale removal
  • Detailed bathroom scrub
  • Inside cabinets (on request)
Great for spring cleaning, moving in, or refreshing neglected spaces.

End‑of‑Tenancy Clean

A full, detailed clean designed to meet landlord and agency standards.

Includes:

  • Full kitchen degrease
  • Oven & hob deep clean
  • Inside all cupboards
  • Bathroom descaling
  • Walls spot‑cleaned
  • Debris removal
  • Paint splatter
  • HVAC dusting
  • Floor polishing
  • Window detailing
  • Final walk-through
Floors, carpets, and surfaces restored to move‑out condition, ideal for tenants, landlords, and property managers.
