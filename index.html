How it works

KaraKleanz is a premiere commercial and domestic cleaning service. We redefine the cleaning experience into an art of spatial sanctity.

Pick Service

Choose from: Carpet Clean, Regular Clean, Deep Clean or End of Tenancy. Each service includes a clear checklist so you always know what’s covered.

Book Slot

Select a date and time that works for you. You’ll receive a confirmation email and reminders before your appointment.

We Arrive

Your cleaner shows up on time with professional products and equipment. All staff are vetted, trained, and insured for your peace of mind.

We Clean

We follow your chosen checklist, focus on your priorities, and leave your space fresh, tidy, and spotless.

Review & Rebook

Once the job is done, you can rate your clean, request adjustments, or book recurring visits.

Services

Carpet Clean Price

Restore the look, feel, and freshness of your carpets with our professional service, leaving your carpets noticeably fresher.

Includes:

  • Powerful hot‑water extraction
  • Stain treatment for spills, marks, and high‑traffic areas
  • Odour removal (pets, smoke, damp)
  • Fast‑drying process
  • Safe, child and pet‑friendly
Perfect for reviving stained carpets, creating a fresher space.

Regular Clean Price

A thorough top‑to‑bottom clean designed to keep your home fresh, tidy, and comfortable.

Includes:

  • Dusting all surfaces
  • Carpet cleaning
  • Vacuuming & mopping floors
  • Kitchen wipe‑down
  • Bathroom clean & sanitisation
  • Emptying bins
  • Light tidying
Perfect for busy households, professionals, and anyone who wants a consistently clean space.

Deep Clean Price

A more intensive clean that targets built‑up dirt and grime, and hard‑to‑reach areas.

Includes everything in the Regular Clean, plus:

  • Skirting boards & door frames
  • Behind appliances (where accessible)
  • Limescale removal
  • Detailed bathroom scrub
  • Inside cabinets (on request)
Great for spring cleaning, moving in, or refreshing neglected spaces.

End of Tenancy Clean Price

A fully detailed clean that's designed to meet landlord and agency standards.

Includes everything in the Deep Clean, plus:

  • Full kitchen degrease
  • Oven & hob deep clean
  • Inside all cupboards
  • Bathroom descaling
  • Debris removal
  • Paint splatter
  • HVAC dusting
  • Floor polishing
  • Window detailing
  • Final walk-through
Great for leaving tenants, landlords, and property managers.
